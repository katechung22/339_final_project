Abilities

  • Hustle: Increases the ability-bearer's Attack by 50%, however, it also reduces the Accuracy of all Physical moves by 20%.
  • Serene Grace: Doubles the chance of moves' secondary effects occurring - specifically stat changes, status ailments, or flinching. It also increases the chance of flinching due to the held items King's Rock and Razor Fang from 10% to 20%.
  • Super Luck: Raises the ability-bearer's critical-hit ratio by one stage.
